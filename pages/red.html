Fiery Aggression - Red

In Magic, Red is all about aggression and activeness. Red's playstyles revolve around sending damage to your opponent's face as fast as possible, and a player that loses that momentum loses Red's power with it.

Red cards will either pepper your opponent relentlessly or uttery demolish them in a single blow. Here are the a few of the most prevalent attributes of a Red playstyle:

  • Amassing a horde of weak creatures to throw at the enemy
  • Sacrificing creatures and other resources quickly to achieve immediate results
  • Keeping the pressure going until a powerful spell or creature is put into play, ensuring a devastating victory

Like all other colors, Red has creature effects and spells that are rarely if ever found with other colors, such as:

  • Dealing damage to any target that takes damage, from creatures to your opponent to yourself
  • Creatures that can attack immediately
  • Creatures that can become very powerful for a single turn
  • Destroying opponents' land cards

If going into battle fast and furiously is for you, then so is Red!
