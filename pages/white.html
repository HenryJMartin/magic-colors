Prosperous Order - White

In Magic, White cards are all about law, order, bravery, and heroics. While at a glance, it's basically the “good” color, playing a White deck can certainly employ a lot of ruthless tactics.

White cards are the best at locking down your opponent's side of the field, leaving them vulnerable to a powerful counterattack. Here are the a some of the more notable attributes of a White playstyle:

  • Cards with lots of rules and conditions, but powerful effects
  • Highly defensive strategies that favor strategic offensive pushes
  • Being able to stay in the fight very easily

Like all other colors, White has creature effects and spells that are rarely if ever found with other colors, such as:

  • Gaining life via spells and creature effects
  • Permanently removing opponent's permanents
  • Giving your creatures various powerful effects with enchantment spells
  • Tapping opponent's creatures to stall them, and tapping your own creatures to trigger strong abilities

If you want a good mix of sustainability and raw power, White is a solid choice!
