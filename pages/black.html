Ruthless Ambition - Black

In Magic, Black cards are often characterized by monsters, vampires, zombies, and generally unpleasant things. While not technically an “evil” color, here you’ll find the most cards and playstyles that fall on the negative side of the moral spectrum.

Black is all about using your spells and creatures in unorthodox, aggressive ways to achieve powerful results. Here are the a few of the most prevalent attributes of a Black playstyle:

  • Removing threats quickly and efficiently
  • Using your creatures, as well as artifacts and even cards in your hand, as resources in addition to mana
  • Being extremely stubborn, always finding a way to stay in the fight a little bit longer

Like all other colors, Black has creature effects and spells that are rarely if ever found with other colors, such as:

  • Draining your opponent’s life total directly
  • Destroying your opponent’s creatures without dealing damage
  • Bringing your own creatures back from the graveyard
  • Getting to see your opponent’s hand

If you want to play a game of schemes, ruthlessness and resilience, Black is the color for you!
